## Featured Project

### Zomato Restaurant Analysis and Expansion Dashboard

**Business question**  
Which Indian cities offer the best restaurant quality, strongest value, and highest expansion potential for Zomato?

**What I built**

- Cleaned and analyzed `9,551` restaurant records across `15` countries
- Focused the analysis on India for pricing, ratings, cuisines, and delivery adoption
- Wrote SQL queries for ranking, filtering, and opportunity analysis
- Built a Tableau dashboard for expansion decision-making
- Created Python visuals and export-ready datasets

**Key insights**

- `Lucknow`, `Guwahati`, and `Ahmedabad` outperform Delhi on ratings
- Online delivery improves engagement more than food quality
- `Italian cuisine` leads average ratings in the India slice
- Delhi street food delivers the strongest value-for-money
- `Ahmedabad` stands out as a high-potential expansion city
